# Privacy Policy — Science Sprouts

**Last updated: 2 June 2026**

Science Sprouts ("the app") is an offline educational science app for children
aged 3–12, published by Sifiso Cyprian Shezi (ARISAN Sifiso Technology Education).

## The short version

**Science Sprouts does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal
information. It has no accounts, no ads, and no analytics. Everything happens
on your device, fully offline.**

## What we collect

**Nothing.** We do not ask for names, email addresses, ages, photos, location,
contacts, or any other personal data. The app never asks a child or a caregiver
to sign in or create an account.

## Information stored on your device

To remember a child's progress, the app saves a small amount of data **only on
your own device** using the browser's local storage (IndexedDB / localStorage):

- Which band (age level) was last selected
- Which activities have been completed and which badges were earned
- Garden / progress visuals

This information **never leaves the device**. It is not uploaded to any server,
not shared with us, and not shared with any third party. Uninstalling the app or
clearing its storage permanently deletes it.

## Internet and network use

The app runs entirely offline and makes no network requests. It does not load
remote content, ads, or trackers. (The Android package includes the standard
`INTERNET` permission that ships by default with the app framework, but the app
does not use it to send or receive any data.)

## Voice narration

Activities can be read aloud using your device's built-in text-to-speech engine.
This is handled by Android on your device. No audio is recorded, stored, or
transmitted by Science Sprouts.

## Third-party services

There are none. The app contains no advertising networks, no analytics SDKs, no
social media SDKs, and no third-party data-collection libraries.

## Children's privacy

Science Sprouts is designed for children and complies with the Google Play
Families Policy. Because the app collects no personal information of any kind, it
collects no information from children.
